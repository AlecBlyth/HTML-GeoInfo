India

India is a former British colony formerly known as British Raj (Which contained Pakistan, India, Bangladesh and Burma)

India gained its independence shortly after World War 2, despite their frustrations with the British Empire; they served and fought alongside British soldiers in both The Great War and World War 2.
India was ruled by the British Empire for hundreds of years and was mainly an economic and trade colony. 


During British rule, in 1909, the fourth largest railway network in the world was constructed. 
India is the second most populous nation, behind China and has estimation of 1.2 Billion citizens and their currency is the Indian Rupee 
The size of India is 3,287,263km2 or 1,269,219 square miles
India is 90.08% land and 9.92% water
India borders, Bangladesh, China, Pakistan, Nepal, Myanmar and Bhutan.


India’s highest point is Kangchenjunga which is located at the Nepal – Indian Border and is over 28,000 ft 
India’s largest lake is the Chilika Lake which is 64.3 km
