Belarus

Belarus is a former Soviet constituent republic which was formerly known as Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, this consisted of Belarus and Poland.

The population of Belarus has around 9 million and their currency is the Belarusian ruble

During World War 2, The Third Reich (Nazi Germany) began Operation Barbarossa, which main objective was the annexation of the Soviet Union. This resulted in the full annexation of Poland and Belarus which later formed the shortly lived Greater Germanic Reich

The total area of Belarus is 207,565km2 or 80,155 square miles. Belarus is a land locked nation and only 1.4% of the total area is water.

Belarus’ neighbours are Russia, Latvia, Lithuania, Poland and Ukraine.

The Highest point is Dzyarzhynskaya hara which is a 345 meter hill

Chernobyl Disaster
After the 1986 notorious disaster in Soviet Ukraine 60% of the radiation spewed was carried by a wind to Belarus which affected 25% of the country and 22% of the population.
